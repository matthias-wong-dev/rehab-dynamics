Rehabilitation solutions

Our Services

We support workers compensation, CTP, NDIS, Support At Home, DVA and chronic disease management clients with assessment, planning and rehabilitation services.

Activities of Daily Living assessment and retraining

Assessment of independent living tasks including meal preparation, shopping, personal hygiene, housework, money management and telephone use.

  • Task modification and equipment recommendations
  • Home modification or care assistance advice
  • Practical strategies for safe independent living

Return-to-work injury management

For work-related injuries and motor vehicle accidents, we develop tailored plans for safe and sustainable return to work.

  • Initial assessment and goal setting
  • Workplace and ergonomic assessment
  • Medical case conference, vocational and transferable skills assessment

Functional Capacity Evaluation

We determine physical capacity against the functional demands of a pre-injury or target role.

  • Cardiovascular and musculoskeletal review
  • Postural tolerance, lifting and carrying capacity
  • Functional recommendations linked to work demands

Workplace and ergonomic assessment

We assess the workplace, role demands and barriers to support a safe office or site-based return to work.

  • Desk, chair and equipment setup advice
  • Psychological and physical role requirement analysis
  • Communication with treating practitioners about work adjustments

Vocational and transferable skills assessment

When returning to pre-injury duties is not realistic, we help identify suitable work options.

  • Review of interests, education and work history
  • Labour market research
  • Training and job-seeking recommendations

Occupational therapy functional assessment

Holistic assessment for Home Care Packages, Chronic Disease Management, NDIS and DVA clients.

  • How daily tasks are completed
  • Support needs and safety risks
  • External supports, equipment and capacity building recommendations
